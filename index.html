Frequently Asked Questions

- What does SkyMed Essentials cover?
SkyMed Essentials covers emergency medical evacuations, hospital transfers, and return transportation within North America and the Caribbean. It also includes coverage for your entire family with no co-pays, no deductibles, and the freedom to choose where to be treated.
+ How is SkyMed Essentials different from travel insurance?
SkyMed Essentials focuses on emergency medical transportation and repatriation, while travel insurance typically covers trip cancellations, delays, and medical expenses.
+ Are there any age restrictions for coverage?
No, SkyMed Essentials does not have age restrictions for its coverage, making it accessible to travelers of all ages.
+ What happens if I need to use SkyMed Essentials?
Simply call the 24/7 emergency hotline. SkyMed will coordinate your medical transportation and other covered services immediately.
+ Can I add my family to the plan?
Yes, you can add family members to your plan for an additional fee to ensure they are also covered.
